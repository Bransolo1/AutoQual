# Incident Response & Breach Notification (Template)

## Detection
- Monitoring alerts and anomaly detection
- Audit log review

## Triage
- Severity classification (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- Identify affected services and data

## Containment
- Revoke tokens and rotate credentials
- Isolate impacted systems

## Eradication & recovery
- Patch vulnerability
- Restore services from verified backups
- Validate service health

## Notification
- Internal notification within 1 hour for Critical
- Customer notification within contract timelines
- Post-incident report within 5 business days
